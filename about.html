The Art of Attar Making

Attar, also known as ittar, is a traditional perfume oil derived from botanical sources. Our master perfumers use ancient hydro-distillation techniques to extract the pure essence of rare flowers, woods, and spices. Each attar is aged for months to develop its full character and depth.


At #WE ARE MUSLIM, we believe that fragrance is a form of worship and self-expression. Our attars are designed to uplift the spirit, enhance focus during prayer, and create lasting memories.
